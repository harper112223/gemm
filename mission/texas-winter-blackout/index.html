Phase 1:

Disaster Relief
Global Empowerment Mission BStrong is responding in the Texas Blackout first phase, disaster relief, by supplying..
 

Family Necessities Kit contain:

  • 10 LBS bag of rice
  • Assortment of 1-3 bags of beans with a total value of 2 LBS
  • 1-2 LBS bag of oats
  • Assortment of protein bars
  • Immuse Immune Boosters
  • Hydration Kits
  • Hygiene supplies (dry shampoo, pack of bars of soap, etc.)
  • 5 masks, gloves & large hand sanitizer
